Our Story

Where Every Meal
Tells a Story

Zaitoon was born from a passion for authentic Arabian flavors — slow-cooked, wood-fired, and crafted with spices that carry centuries of culinary tradition.

Nestled in Royapettah, Chennai, we've built a dining destination that seamlessly blends Arabian, Indian, and Chinese cuisines under one roof. A space where families gather, food lovers discover, and late-night cravings find their home.

Every dish we serve is a testament to our commitment: real ingredients, real technique, real flavor.
